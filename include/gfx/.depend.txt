Here are the user terms and the dependency tree of this package.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
 F - Full-process usage, maybe, no need to dispose.                        
 T - Temporary usage. Users must dispose the resource after using.
 V - Value-only, meaning a class won't and shouldn't be packed in pointers.
 U - Unique-ptr usage.
 R - Raw-ptr usage.
 S - Shared-ptr usage.
 W - Weak-ptr -usage.
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
